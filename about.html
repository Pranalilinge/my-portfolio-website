My story

Hi, I’m Pranali Linge, and I’m a UX/UI designer with experience in Industrial design. During my tenure as an industrial designer has thought me how the world is enhancing from PHYSICAL to DIGITAL product. This virtual adaptability is what my customer wants, and this user-centric philosophy is what led me to user experience design. I'm constantly inspired by artists and dreamers, who are daring and limitless, and it is my personal challenge to push my own boundaries of creativity at work and grow as a designer. I believe that the aim of design in any form is to make life comfortable and easier, and to achieve this my products must be smart and simple. Now, I’m looking forward to applying the same principles to designing user flows and brands that make a positive impact on people’s lives and make digital products easier and more intuitive to use. Outside of work I spend my time taking photos, traveling, and designing wooden toys. I’m currently available for full-time work. If you want to say hi, please get in touch via pranaliplinge@gmail.com.
