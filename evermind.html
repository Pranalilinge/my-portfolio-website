Brief

With Evermind, you can find quick audio and video guides of exercises for anxiety, sleep issues, stress, and boosting your self-esteem.

Challenge

In light of the overall growth in virtual activities since 2020, along with the damaging impact that COVID-19 has had on individual mental health, I wanted to contribute to a product space that can assist users in acquiring and maintaining mental fitness.

Role

UX research, UX design, Visual design

Duration

5 months

Tools

  • Figma
  • Adobe illustrator
  • Adobe photoshop

      User research

      United the key insights from the research of competitor apps and observational data from the user interviews to determine which direction to take with my project. I decided to ideate and design an application that directs the user through audio and video guides and talk to an expert. I developed personas to represent the needs, goals and behaviors of the potential users.

      user journey map

      Next, I mapped my target users’ common emotions when completing tasks. Through this process I gained a deeper understanding of how emotions such as frustration or confusion could lead to decreased trust in the product or abandonment of a task altogether.
